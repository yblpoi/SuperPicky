01

快速开始Quick Start

先把第一批照片顺利跑完，再慢慢学会更高级的功能。Run your first batch successfully, then gradually learn the advanced features.

1
Overview

1.1软件能帮你做什么What the Software Does

慧眼选鸟最核心的作用，是把大量鸟片先快速初筛，再把结果整理得更清楚，最后在需要时帮你识别鸟种。它不是替你做最后决定，而是先把最耗时间的第一轮工作做掉。

对大多数观鸟摄影师来说，最费时间的通常不是拍摄，而是回家后面对几百张甚至上千张相似照片不知道从哪里开始。慧眼选鸟的目标，就是先把这些照片分出优先级，让你先看最值得看的那部分。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先把软件理解成“帮你初筛”的工具，而不是“代替你审美”的工具。Think of it as a pre-screening tool, not a replacement for your aesthetic judgement.
  2. 处理完成后，优先查看高星照片和重点标记照片。After processing, start with the highest-rated and flagged photos.
  3. 把它当作第一轮整理助手，最后保留哪张仍由你决定。Treat it as your first-round sorting assistant — the final keep/delete decision is always yours.
💡 第一次使用时，不要想着把所有功能一次学完，先做到“能顺利跑完一批照片”就已经足够。On your first use, don't try to learn every feature. Successfully completing one batch is enough.

这一节学完后，你应该知道After this section you should understand

软件负责先缩小范围，你负责最后判断哪张最值得留下。The software narrows the field; you make the final judgement on which photos to keep.

最适合的使用场景Best use cases

  • 一次拍了很多张，不知道从哪里开始删。You shot a lot and don't know where to begin culling.
  • 连拍很多，很多照片看起来差不多。You burst-shot heavily and many frames look almost identical.
  • 既想选片，也想顺便知道拍到的是什么鸟。You want to cull and identify species at the same time.
2
Prepare

1.2开始前需要准备什么What to Prepare

第一次练习时，请先准备一个内容比较单纯的照片文件夹，比如同一天、同一地点或同一次短途外拍的照片。这样最容易看懂结果，也不容易被复杂目录搞乱。

软件支持常见的 RAW、JPG，以及部分 HEIF、HEIC、HIF 格式。第一次不建议直接处理整趟旅行总目录，更不建议一上来就选一个层级很多的大目录，因为这样出问题时不容易排查。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先找一批你最近拍的鸟片，数量适中即可。Find a recent batch of bird photos — a moderate number is fine.
  2. 优先选择同一次拍摄的照片目录，不要混太多无关内容。Choose a folder from a single shoot; don't mix in unrelated content.
  3. 确认里面主要是你想处理的照片，而不是混着视频、文档或其他杂项。Confirm the folder mainly contains photos you want processed, not videos, documents, or other files.
  4. 如果目录很大，先从一个子目录开始练习。If the folder is large, start with a subdirectory for practice.
📁 第一遍建议拿“你不怕试错”的照片练习，不要一上来就处理最重要的正式成片目录。Practice with photos you don't mind experimenting on — don't start with your most important finals folder.

推荐第一次练习的目录Good folders to practice with

  • 一个拍摄日期的文件夹。A single shooting-day folder.
  • 一个机位或一个早晨拍到的照片。Photos from one spot or one morning session.
  • 数量在自己能接受的范围内，不必追求多。A manageable number of photos — quantity is not the goal.

先不要做的事What to avoid for now

先不要直接选整个硬盘、整趟旅行总目录，或层级特别深的大目录。Don't select your entire drive, a whole-trip root folder, or a deeply nested hierarchy.

First Run

1.5第一次完整跑一遍选鸟

第一次正式操作时，请尽量简单。先用默认参数跑一遍，看看软件的判断风格，再决定后面要不要微调。Keep your first real run simple. Use the default settings, see how the app judges your photos, then decide whether to fine-tune.

大多数新手第一次出问题，不是因为软件太难，而是因为一开始就改了太多设置，导致自己也不知道哪里改坏了。先跑通默认流程，后面会轻松很多。Most beginners run into trouble not because the app is hard, but because they changed too many settings at once. Run the defaults first — everything becomes easier after that.

  5. 想顺便识别鸟种时打开“识鸟”。Enable "Bird ID" if you want species identification alongside culling.
  6. 其他参数先不动，直接点击“开始处理”。Leave other settings as-is and click "Start Processing".
  7. 处理结束后点击“查看结果”。Click "View Results" when processing finishes.
✅ 第一次的目标不是把设置调到最完美，而是先顺利得到一套能看懂的结果。Your goal for the first run isn't perfect settings — it's getting a readable set of results to learn from.

记住一个原则One principle to remember

第一次的目标不是把设置调到最完美，而是先顺利跑完整个流程，看到结果，再决定下次要不要调整。

02

如何设置与开始选鸟Settings & Starting a Cull

先用对思路，再谈调参数。绝大多数结果问题，都能在这一章找到原因。Get the mindset right before tweaking parameters. Most result issues can be traced back to this chapter.

1
Skill Level

2.1摄影水平怎么选Choosing Your Skill Level

摄影水平本质上是几套不同的默认标准。它不是在评价你的技术，而是在决定这次选片要宽松一点，还是严格一点。Skill level is essentially a set of different default criteria. It is not a judgement of your technique — it determines whether this cull should be lenient or strict.

新手模式删得最少，适合不想漏掉任何片子的情况；初级模式删除力度居中，最平衡，适合大多数用户；大师模式更严格，更适合已经非常清楚自己想留下什么的人。三者的核心差别就是删除力度，拿不准就选「初级」。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 如果你刚开始拍鸟，或不想删得太狠，选“新手”。If you're new to bird photography or want to keep more photos, choose "Beginner".
  2. 如果你想用最稳妥的默认方案，选“初级”。For the safest default approach, choose "Intermediate".
  3. 如果你本来删片就很严格，只想快速留下精品，选“大师”。If you already cull aggressively and want only the best, choose "Master".
  4. 跑完一批后，再根据结果多少决定下次是否要更严格或更宽松。After running a batch, decide whether to go stricter or looser based on how many photos were kept.
🎯 如果你拿不准，优先选“初级”。绝大多数用户第一次都适合从这里开始。When in doubt, choose "Intermediate". Most users should start here.

怎么判断要不要改档How to decide whether to switch levels

  • 留下来的太多，下次就更严格一点。Too many kept — go stricter next time.
  • 留下来的太少，下次就更宽松一点。Too few kept — go looser next time.
  • 第一次先别频繁来回切换。On your first run, don't keep switching back and forth.
2
Thresholds

2.2常用参数怎么理解Understanding Key Parameters

最重要的几个参数，核心都在决定一件事：软件认为什么算“足够好”。参数高，不代表更高级，只代表更严格。

锐度阈值主要影响清晰度标准，美学阈值主要影响整体观感标准，AI 检测敏感度会影响软件判断目标时的门槛。第一次如果还不知道自己到底不满意什么，就先不要急着改。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 把锐度阈值理解成“清晰度门槛”。Think of the sharpness threshold as the clarity bar.
  2. 把美学阈值理解成“整体画面门槛”。Think of the aesthetics threshold as the overall image quality bar.
  3. 把 AI 检测敏感度理解成“软件判断目标的门槛”。Think of the AI detection sensitivity as the bar for how confident the app must be to detect a subject.
  4. 先看默认结果，再决定是不是要微调。Check the default results first, then decide whether to fine-tune.
⚙️ 如果你还说不清“这次结果哪里不满意”，就先不要调参数。If you can't clearly say what's wrong, don't touch the parameters yet.

三个最常见误区Three most common mistakes

  • 参数越高不代表越专业。Higher parameters do not mean more professional.
  • 第一次就大幅修改参数，通常更容易迷糊。Changing many parameters on your first run usually causes more confusion.
  • 不满意时先判断是结果太多，还是太少，再动参数。When unsatisfied, first decide if there are too many or too few results before adjusting parameters.
3
Feature Toggles

2.3飞版、连拍、识鸟什么时候开启When to Enable BIF / Burst / Bird ID

这三个功能不需要每次都全开。最好的方法，是根据你这批照片的内容来决定，而不是为了“功能更全”而全部打开。

飞版适合飞鸟很多的拍摄；连拍适合相似照片很多的拍摄；识鸟适合你希望顺便知道鸟种，或者后面想按鸟种筛选和整理的情况。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 静栖鸟为主，可以先不开飞版。Mostly perched birds — you can skip BIF for now.
  2. 飞鸟很多，建议开飞版。Lots of birds in flight — enable BIF.
  3. 一组动作经常拍出很多相似照片，建议开连拍。Frequently burst-shooting similar action frames — enable Burst.
  4. 只想先快速初筛，可以先不开识鸟。Just want a quick first pass — skip Bird ID for now.
  5. 想知道拍到什么鸟，或后面想按鸟种看结果，打开识鸟。Want to know what species you shot, or plan to filter by species later — enable Bird ID.
🧩 开关不是越多越好，而是越贴近这批照片的实际内容越好。More toggles is not better — the right choice fits the actual content of this batch.

最常见的三种组合Three most common feature combinations

  • 静栖鸟：连拍按需开，识鸟按需开。Perched birds: enable Burst and Bird ID as needed.
  • 飞鸟多：飞版 + 连拍。Lots of flight shots: BIF + Burst.
  • 想完整整理：连拍 + 识鸟，再按情况加飞版。Full workflow: Burst + Bird ID, add BIF as needed.
4
Progress

2.4处理过程中常见提示怎么看Reading the Progress Log

处理时最重要的，不是看懂每一条日志，而是判断软件是否在正常往前走。大多数时候，你只需要看日志是不是持续刷新。During processing, what matters most is not understanding every log line, but confirming that the app is moving forward. Most of the time, just check that new lines keep appearing.

日志区会显示当前正在检测、分析、整理、识别等信息。照片越多、功能越多，处理时间越长，这是正常现象，不代表出问题。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 点击开始处理后，先看日志区是否持续出现新内容。After clicking Start Processing, watch whether new lines keep appearing in the log.
  2. 看到“检测”“分析”“整理”等词，说明流程在继续。Seeing words like "detecting", "analysing", "organising" means the process is running.
  3. 如果中间某一步停留稍久，先不要急着关掉。If one step takes a while, don't close the app — wait it out.
  4. 如果超过 5 分钟日志完全没有新内容出现，再考虑是否卡住或出错。If no new log entries appear for over 5 minutes, then consider whether it may be stuck.
⏱️ 你不需要逐字看懂日志，只要知道“它还在动，而且没有报错”就够了。You don't need to understand every log line — just confirm it's moving and not showing errors.

先不要慌的情况Situations where you should stay calm

  • 照片很多。You have a large number of photos.
  • 连拍和识鸟都开了。Both Burst and Bird ID are enabled.
  • 某一步暂时花的时间比较长。One step is taking longer than usual.
5
Recommended Defaults

2.5新手最推荐的默认用法Recommended Defaults for Beginners

如果你不想一开始被设置搞复杂，这里有一套最稳妥的起步方案。它不是唯一正确答案，但通常最适合第一次真正上手。If you don't want to be overwhelmed by settings at the start, here is the safest starting configuration. It is not the only right answer, but it is almost always the best choice for a first real run.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 目录只选一个不大的拍摄文件夹。Choose just one reasonably sized shooting folder.
  2. 摄影水平选“初级”。Set skill level to "Intermediate".
  3. 打开“连拍”。Enable "Burst".
  4. 飞鸟很多就开“飞版”。Enable "BIF" if there are many birds in flight.
  5. 想知道鸟种就开“识鸟”，否则先不开也可以。Enable "Bird ID" if you want species info, otherwise skip it for now.
  6. 其他参数先保持默认。Leave all other settings at their defaults.
  7. 跑完以后，先看高星结果，再决定下次要不要微调。After the run, check high-star results first, then decide whether to fine-tune next time.
👍 先把默认流程跑熟，再谈个性化设置，效率最高。Get comfortable with the default workflow before customising settings — that order is most efficient.

为什么推荐这样用Why this approach is recommended

这样最容易分清：到底是软件判断的问题，还是你还没习惯它的工作方式。This makes it easiest to tell whether the issue is the app's judgement or you still getting familiar with how it works.

如何看懂选鸟结果Reading Cull Results

先学会“怎么读结果”，再决定“怎么改结果”，效率会高很多。Learn to read the results before deciding how to change them - much more efficient.

1
Ratings

3.10 星到 3 星分别代表什么What 0-3 Stars Mean

星级是软件给你的第一轮筛选结果。最简单的理解方法是：3 星优先看，2 星值得保留候选，1 星是普通记录片，0 星通常不建议优先保留。Star ratings are the app's first-pass results. The simplest way to think about them: 3-star = see first; 2-star = worth keeping as candidates; 1-star = ordinary documentary shots; 0-star = usually not worth prioritising.

看结果时，不要从全部照片重新翻起，而是先从高星往下看。这样最符合这款软件的使用逻辑，也最省时间。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先把 3 星理解成“优先重点看”。Think of 3-star as "priority — see these first".
  2. 再把 2 星理解成“质量不错，值得复查”。Think of 2-star as good quality, worth reviewing.
  3. 1 星放在后面看，通常作为记录片参考。Review 1-star last — these are typically documentary reference shots.
  4. 0 星最后再看，先不要在这里花太多时间。Look at 0-star last; don't spend much time here initially.
⭐ 星级的意义是帮你省时间，不是强迫你接受结果。自己特别喜欢的片子，当然可以留。Star ratings save you time — they don't force a result. You can always keep a photo you love.

最实用的浏览顺序Most practical review order

  • 先看 3 星。Start with 3-star.
  • 再看 2 星。Then 2-star.
  • 最后才看 1 星和 0 星。Finally 1-star and 0-star.

  3. 切换后观察是否更符合你的使用习惯。After switching, check whether the names match your usual workflow.
  4. 长期使用时尽量保持一致，不要频繁切换。Stay consistent over time — avoid switching standards frequently.
📚 普通用户长期用默认最省心，真正需要统一名录时再切换即可。For most users, sticking with the default is easiest — only switch when you need a consistent formal checklist.

选择标准时最重要的事The most important thing when choosing a standard

不是选“哪一个最好”，而是选“你以后一直想沿用哪一个”。

6
Screenshot ID

5.6截图识鸟怎么用Using Screenshot Bird ID

截图识鸟适合你临时看到一只鸟，想快速试试识别，但手上没有原始照片的时候使用。它更适合做快速参考，不如直接处理原图稳定。Screenshot Bird ID is for situations where you spot a bird and want a quick ID but do not have the original file. It is better suited for quick reference than for reliable identification — processing original files is more accurate.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 打开截图识鸟功能。Open the Screenshot Bird ID feature.
  2. 按提示框选你要识别的鸟。Follow the prompt to draw a selection box around the bird you want to identify.
  3. 等待软件给出候选结果。Wait for the app to return candidate results.
  4. 如果结果不理想，换一张更清楚、更完整的截图再试。If results are unsatisfactory, try a clearer or more complete screenshot.
  5. 在 macOS 上，如果用不了，前往「系统设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 屏幕录制」，确认已勾选 SuperPicky。On macOS, if it does not work, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording and confirm SuperPicky is checked.
🖼️ 截图识鸟适合快速参考，不适合替代完整原图识别流程。Screenshot Bird ID is good for quick reference; it should not replace processing original files.

什么时候截图识鸟最有用When Screenshot Bird ID is most useful

  • 在网页上看到一张鸟图想试着认。You spot a bird photo online and want a quick ID.
  • 别人发来一张图，想快速有个参考答案。Someone sends you a photo and you want a quick reference answer.
  • 暂时没有原始照片，只想先看看候选结果。You have no original file and just want a candidate result.
06

批量处理、重置与常见问题Batch Processing, Reset & Troubleshooting

照片一多，真正重要的不是更复杂，而是更稳、更清楚、更不容易把目录弄乱。At scale, what matters is stability, clarity, and not accidentally making a mess.

1
Recursive Batch

6.1递归处理子目录怎么用Recursive Batch Processing

如果你的照片不是都放在一个文件夹里，而是按日期、地点或机位分了很多层目录，递归处理会很省时间。但第一次用时，范围一定要保守一点。If your photos are split across many date, location, or session subfolders, recursive processing saves a lot of time. But on your first use, always start with a conservative scope.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先找到这次要处理的总目录。First locate the root folder you want to process this time.
  2. 确认它下面确实就是你这次想处理的那些子目录。Confirm that the subfolders inside are exactly the ones you intend to process.
  3. 启用递归处理，让软件自动往下扫描。Enable recursive processing so the app scans all subfolders automatically.
  4. 第一次先选一个只有几十到几百张照片的子目录试，不要直接扫太大的总目录。For your first try, select a subfolder with only tens or hundreds of photos — avoid scanning a huge root folder right away.
  5. 确认流程没问题后，再扩大范围。Once you confirm everything works correctly, expand to a larger scope.
🌲 第一次用递归处理时，宁可少一点，也不要一下选太大。On first use of recursive processing, start small — conservative scope is always safer.

最适合用递归处理的情况Best situations for recursive batch processing

  • 照片按日期分了很多文件夹。Photos are split across many date-based folders.
  • 一趟旅行分成很多小目录。A single trip is organised into many small subfolders.
  • 你不想一个个目录手动重复操作。You don't want to manually process each folder one by one.
2
Recent Directories

6.2最近目录与重复处理怎么管理Managing Recent Folders

软件会记住最近处理过的目录，这样你下次可以很快回到之前的工作位置。但每次再次打开时，都要先确认是不是你真正想处理的目录。The app remembers recently processed folders so you can quickly return to where you left off. But every time you open the app, confirm it is actually the folder you intend to process this time.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 通过最近目录快速回到之前的工作位置。Use the Recent Directories list to quickly return to a previous working folder.
  2. 每次点开前，先确认路径是不是这次真正要处理的目录。Before opening, confirm the path is actually the folder you intend to process this time.
  3. 如果只是继续看旧结果，不要重复开始处理。If you only want to review old results, do not start processing again.
  4. 如果你想换标准重新来，再考虑重置。If you want to reprocess with different settings, then consider resetting.
📌 养成“先确认目录再动手”的习惯，可以避免很多重复整理带来的混乱。Getting into the habit of confirming the folder before acting avoids a lot of confusion from repeated re-processing.

最容易出错的地方The most common mistake

不是软件本身，而是你以为自己打开的是新目录，其实又点回了旧目录。It's not the app itself — it's thinking you opened a new folder when you actually clicked back into an old one.

3
Reset

6.3什么情况下需要重置When to Reset

重置不是每次都要用。它只适合你想“重新来过”的时候，比如想换一套标准重新处理，或者觉得当前目录结果已经不适合继续沿用。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先问自己：我是只是想继续看，还是想真正重新处理。Ask yourself first: do I just want to keep reviewing, or do I actually want to reprocess?
  2. 如果只是继续看旧结果，就先不要重置。If you only want to keep reviewing old results, do not reset.
  3. 如果想换标准重新来一遍，再执行重置。If you want to reprocess with different criteria, then run the reset.
  4. 执行前先确认，这确实是你要重来的那批照片。Before confirming, verify this is actually the batch you want to redo.
♻️ 不确定时，先不要重置，先看旧结果通常更安全。When in doubt, skip the reset and review old results first — that is usually the safer choice.

可以先不重置的情况Situations where you can skip resetting

  • 你只是想再看一遍旧结果。You just want to review the old results again.
  • 你还没想清楚这次要改什么。You haven't decided yet what to change.
  • 你只是想确认目录当前状态。You just want to check the current state of the folder.
4
Reprocess

6.4重置后如何重新处理Reprocessing After a Reset

重置的意义，是给你一个重新开始的机会。但重置以后，最好带着明确目标重新跑，而不是“什么都不改再来一遍”。

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先完成重置。Complete the reset first.
  2. 想清楚这次是要更严格、还是更宽松，或要不要加识鸟。Decide whether this run should be stricter, looser, or whether to add Bird ID.
  3. 重新设置摄影水平和功能开关。Set the skill level and feature toggles again.
  4. 再开始新一轮处理。Then start the new processing run.
  5. 处理后重点看这次和上次差别最大的地方。After processing, focus on where the results differ most from the previous run.
🛠️ 带着明确目的重新处理，效果通常比盲目重跑好得多。Reprocessing with a clear goal almost always produces better results than rerunning blindly.

最值得先改的通常是The settings most worth changing first

  • 摄影水平。Skill level.
  • 识鸟开关。Bird ID toggle.
  • 这次是否启用飞版或连拍。Whether to enable BIF or Burst this time.
5
Tray

6.5系统托盘与后台运行System Tray and Background Mode

后台运行适合你暂时不想关掉软件，但又不想让它一直占着桌面的时候。最小化到托盘不等于已经退出程序，这一点要特别注意。Background mode is for when you want to step away without closing the app, but do not want it taking up desktop space. Minimising to the tray does not quit the app — this is an important distinction.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 如果只是暂时离开主窗口，可以最小化到托盘。If you just need to step away temporarily, minimise to the system tray.
  2. 需要继续使用时，再从托盘恢复主窗口。When you want to return, restore the main window from the tray.
  3. 如果你彻底不用了，请正常退出程序。If you're done for now, quit the app properly.
  4. 不要把“关窗口”和“退出程序”混为一谈。Do not confuse "closing the window" with "quitting the app".
🖥️ 新手最容易把“隐藏”和“退出”搞混，记住这两件事不是一回事。Beginners often confuse \hiding\ the window with \quitting\ the app — remember they are different things.

一句话理解In one sentence

最小化到托盘，软件还在；正常退出，软件才真正关闭。Minimising to the tray keeps the app running. Only quitting the app properly shuts it down.

6
Recommended Scenarios

6.6新手常见场景推荐设置Recommended Settings by Scenario

最好的设置不是最复杂的，而是最适合你这批照片内容的。不同拍摄场景，适合的开关组合也不一样。The best settings are not the most complex — they are the ones that best fit your current batch. Different shooting scenarios call for different feature combinations.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 静栖鸟为主：先用默认设置，连拍按需开。Mostly perched birds: use default settings, enable Burst as needed.
  2. 飞鸟很多：打开飞版。Many birds in flight: enable BIF.
  3. 连拍很多：打开连拍。Heavy burst shooting: enable Burst.
  4. 想知道鸟种：打开识鸟，并尽量设好国家或地区。Want species info: enable Bird ID and set country/region as accurately as possible.
  5. 电脑性能一般：第一次不要所有功能全开。Average PC performance: do not enable all features at once on your first run.
🧰 先按场景选开关，比一上来研究复杂参数更有效。Choosing toggles based on your shooting scenario is much more effective than researching complex parameters first.

最省心的一套起点The lowest-effort starting point

  • 初级。"Intermediate" skill level.
  • 连拍开。Burst enabled.
  • 飞版和识鸟按内容决定。BIF and Bird ID based on your photo content.

Troubleshooting

6.7常见问题与排查方法Troubleshooting Guide

遇到问题时，先把条件变简单，再排查，通常最有效。最怕的是一次改太多东西，结果自己也不知道问题从哪里来。When something goes wrong, simplify conditions first, then troubleshoot — that is almost always the most effective approach. The worst thing to do is change many things at once and lose track of what caused the problem.

操作步骤Steps
  1. 先看日志区有没有明显报错。First check the log area for any obvious errors.
  2. 确认目录是不是选对了。Confirm you selected the correct folder.
  3. 确认是不是一次开了太多功能。Check whether you enabled too many features at once.
  4. 如果问题出在大目录，先拿一个小目录试。If the problem involves a large folder, reproduce it with a small one first.
  5. 如果识鸟不准，先检查国家或地区设置。If Bird ID results are off, check the country/region setting first.
  6. 如果截图识鸟不能用，先检查权限。If Screenshot Bird ID does not work, check app permissions first.
  7. 排查时先简化条件，不要一口气改很多设置。When troubleshooting, simplify conditions first — don't change many settings at once.
🧪 先把问题缩小，再去解决，通常会比“全都一起改”快得多。Narrowing down the problem first is almost always faster than changing multiple things at once.

一个最稳妥的排查顺序The safest troubleshooting order

  • 先看日志。Check the log first.
  • 再看目录和开关。Then check the folder selection and feature toggles.
  • 再用小目录复现。Reproduce the issue with a small folder.
  • 最后再动参数。Only then adjust parameters.

Once this workflow feels smooth, all the advanced features will come naturally.
